# MindRailo Build 2.0 — Kids World & Revenue Foundation

## Included
- 31 discoverable activity pages for ages 6–11
- Maths, English, Science, Geography, Logic and Classic hubs
- Playable quiz engine, Memory Match, Tic-Tac-Toe and Snakes & Ladders
- Comprehensive age-based Maths and English curriculum framework
- Browser voice selector using every narration voice available on the visitor's device
- Guided Help Teacher with child-safe, pre-reviewed hints
- Local parent progress view
- SEO metadata and sitemap entries for every game
- Reserved AdSense placements and reusable affiliate recommendation modules

## Important limitations
- Browser narration languages depend on voices installed on the visitor's device.
- The Help Teacher is an offline guided-hint system in this static release. It does not send children's data to an external AI service.
- Affiliate links and live ads are not activated until the owner is approved by the relevant programmes and inserts approved IDs/links.
- The curriculum is a global primary-learning framework, not a claim of official accreditation by every country.
